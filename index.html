Ghost Invasion: Idle Hunter

Hunt, Collect, Upgrade – The Ghosts Won't Stop, But Neither Will You!

About the Game

Ghost Invasion: Idle Hunter is an exciting idle game where players take on the role of a skilled ghost hunter tasked with defending the world from a ghostly invasion. Use a variety of weapons, traps, and upgrades to hunt down and capture the invading ghosts. As you progress, you unlock new ghost-hunting tools, abilities, and heroes to assist in your mission. The game offers a mix of strategic decision-making and idle gameplay mechanics, allowing for easy progression while maintaining a sense of challenge.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Hunt Ghosts

    Use your powerful weapons and heroes to hunt down and capture ghosts.

  • Idle Progress

    Your hunters will continue to capture ghosts automatically while you're away, so you can return to find your progress.

  • Upgrade Weapons and Traps

    Upgrade your weapons, traps, and hunters to increase your ghost-hunting efficiency.

  • Unlock New Heroes

    Unlock and recruit unique ghost hunters, each with special abilities to help you defeat stronger enemies.

  • Complete Missions

    Complete various missions and challenges to earn rewards, level up faster, and unlock new areas of the game.

Characters

  • Hunter Jack: The first hunter you recruit, Jack is a jack-of-all-trades. He’s skilled in handling a variety of weapons and can deal moderate damage to all types of ghosts.

  • Evelyn the Witch: A mystical character who uses dark magic to deal heavy damage to ghostly spirits. Her special ability allows her to trap multiple ghosts at once.

  • Gordon the Exorcist: A powerful exorcist who specializes in dealing with the most difficult and rare ghosts. His abilities can banish ghosts with a single strike.

  • Dr. Claire: A scientist who uses advanced technology to trap ghosts. Her special traps deal high area damage and can capture multiple ghosts at once.

  • Danny the Ghostbuster: A tough, no-nonsense ghost hunter who specializes in using heavy-duty weapons. Danny’s ability is perfect for defeating large groups of ghosts in a short period.

FAQ

How do I start hunting ghosts?
Simply download the game, and you’ll begin with your first hunter. Use your available weapons to start capturing ghosts and expand your team.
Can I play Ghost Invasion: Idle Hunter offline?
Yes, the idle mechanics allow the game to progress even when you are not actively playing. However, some features like events and challenges require an internet connection.
How do I unlock new heroes?
Heroes are unlocked as you progress through the game. Complete specific missions or reach milestones to recruit powerful new hunters.
What are boosters?
Boosters are special items that can help you complete challenging levels faster or with more ease.
Are there in-app purchases?
Yes, you can purchase various upgrades and boosters to speed up your progress, but these are optional. The game can be enjoyed without spending money.
What are the ghost types in the game?
There are a variety of ghosts, each with unique abilities and strengths. The further you progress, the more difficult and dangerous the ghosts become.
