About the Game

Punko.io: Tower Defense RPG combines the excitement of tower defense with immersive RPG elements. In this epic game, players must protect their kingdom from waves of enemy creatures by strategically placing towers, upgrading defenses, and unlocking new characters with unique abilities. With hundreds of levels, various towers to choose from, and a rich storyline, Punko.io offers hours of strategic gameplay that will keep you hooked.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Place Towers

    Strategically place different types of towers along enemy paths to prevent waves of enemies from reaching your base.

  • Upgrade Defenses

    Use resources to upgrade your towers and unlock powerful abilities to make them stronger and more effective.

  • Choose Heroes

    Unlock powerful heroes that bring unique skills and combat abilities to help you defend against powerful foes.

  • Explore Worlds

    Progress through diverse fantasy realms, each with unique enemies, challenges, and environmental obstacles.

  • Plan Your Strategy

    Each level demands careful planning – no two levels are the same, so adapt your tactics to the situation.

Characters

Meet the heroes who will help you defend your kingdom!

  • Kael, the Warrior: A strong and durable hero who excels in close combat. He’s perfect for leading the frontlines and tanking damage.

  • Lira, the Sorceress: A powerful mage who can cast devastating spells. Use her magic to clear enemy waves and control the battlefield.

  • Thorn, the Archer: A master of ranged combat, Thorn can take down enemies from afar with precision. Her speed and accuracy make her an essential part of any defense.

  • Astra, the Healer: A support hero who can heal damaged towers and heroes, ensuring your defenses stay strong through tough battles.

  • Grim, the Beast: A monstrous warrior with devastating power. Grim can crush enemies with ease, but his slow movement makes him a high-risk, high-reward hero.

FAQ

How do I unlock new heroes?
New heroes are unlocked as you progress through the game. Complete specific levels or achieve certain milestones to add new heroes to your roster.
Can I play Punko.io offline?
Yes, Punko.io can be played offline, but some features like multiplayer battles and live events require an internet connection.
Are there in-app purchases?
Yes, there are in-app purchases for boosters, skins, and heroes, but the game can be fully enjoyed without spending money.
How do I upgrade my towers?
Use the in-game currency earned from battles to upgrade your towers and unlock new abilities for your heroes.
What’s the multiplayer mode about?
The multiplayer mode allows you to challenge other players' defenses and see how your strategy stacks up against theirs in real-time.
